How to Activate Your UHC Health Plan Online – activate.uhc.com

Activate Now

You can quickly set up your UnitedHealthcare account by visiting activate.uhc.com. Go to the website, enter your member ID, date of birth, and ZIP code, then create a username, password, and security questions. Verify your email or phone number, and once complete, you can log in anytime to view benefits, access your digital ID card, track claims, and manage your health plan online.

Step-by-Step Guide to Activating Your UHC Account

1. Go to activate.uhc.com

Open your browser on your phone, tablet, or computer and type in activate.uhc.com.

2. Enter your member details

Provide your UHC member ID (from your welcome packet or card), date of birth, and ZIP code.

3. Create your login credentials

Choose a username and password, then set up security questions to protect your account.

4. Verify your email or phone number

UHC will send a verification code to confirm your identity.

5. Sign in and explore your plan

Once activated, you can log in anytime to view your benefits, download your ID card, and access digital tools.

It’s UnitedHealthcare’s secure website where new members register their accounts, access ID cards, and view health plan details.

2. Do I need my member ID to activate my account?

Yes, you’ll need your UHC member ID, date of birth, and ZIP code to complete registration at activate.uhc.com.

3. Can I use my phone to activate my UHC plan?

Absolutely. You can visit activate.uhc.com on any device — phone, tablet, or computer — to set up your account in minutes.
